A BATON ROUGE SEAFOOD CULTURE

JaZee Seafood is a family-run business in Baton Rouge, Louisiana, with approximately 100 years of experience. We were born and raised in this area, so it has a special place in our hearts. Experience our tried-and-true Southern hospitality by joining us for a seafood feast. It is an honor for JaZee Seafood Restaurant to serve Baton Rouge and its guests. We anticipate the future with the expectation that it will only get better!
